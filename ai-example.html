Manual Controls:

  • Arrow keys or A/D to move paddle
  • Mouse or touch to move paddle
  • Press I to toggle AI control

AI Functions (available in console):

  • gameAPI.enableAIControl(true) - Enable AI
  • gameAPI.setAIPaddleX(200) - Set paddle position
  • gameAPI.moveAIPaddleLeft(50) - Move paddle left
  • gameAPI.moveAIPaddleRight(50) - Move paddle right
  • gameAPI.getGameState() - Get current game state
  • gameAPI.predictBallHitPaddle() - Predict ball trajectory
